## How to Add Your Photos

1. **Rename your photos** to match these names:
   - `photo1.jpg`
   - `photo2.jpg`
   - `photo3.jpg`

2. **Place the photos** in the same folder as `index.html`

3. **Supported formats**: `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.gif`, `.webp`

## If You Want Different Photo Names

You can keep your original photo names! Just open `index.html` and update these lines:

```html
<img src="photo1.jpg" alt="Our moment 1">
```

Change `photo1.jpg` to your actual filename, like `vacation.jpg` or `our-date.png`

## Photo Tips for Best Results

- **Recommended size**: 800x800 pixels or larger
- **Aspect ratio**: Square or portrait works best
- **File size**: Keep under 2MB each for faster loading
